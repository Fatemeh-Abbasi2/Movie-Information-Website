Jenna Ortega

American actress and voice actress Jenna Ortega was born on September 27, 2002 in Coachella Valley, California. Besides acting, she is also interested in writing, her book was published in January 2022 with the name (It's All Love). Jenna Ortega has a history of acting and voicing in films and series such as Wednesday, Scream 5, Yes Day, Princess Elena, Jurassic World: Camp Cretaceous, Saving Flora, You and Iron Man 3 in her artistic career.
